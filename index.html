WHO IS VIRAT KOHLI?


Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world.
Born on November 5, 1988 in Delhi, India.
Kohli has achieved numerous records and accolades throughout his cricket career.
Virat Kohli showed an early interest in cricket and started playing at a young age.
He rose through the ranks of Delhi's age-group cricket and eventually made his first-class debut for Delhi in 2006
Kohli took over the captaincy of the Indian Test team in 2014, and he became the captain of the limited-overs teams in 2017.
Under his leadership, India achieved significant successes, including historic Test series wins in Australia.
Virat Kohli has been associated with the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL) since the tournament's inception in 2008.
He has also served as the captain of RCB.Kohli has received numerous awards for his performances, including the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, India's highest sporting honor.
He has been named the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times.
Apart from cricket, Kohli is known for his philanthropic work.
He has been involved in various charitable initiatives, including the Virat Kohli Foundation, which aims to support underprivileged children.
